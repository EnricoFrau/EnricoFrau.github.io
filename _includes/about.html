My Expertise

I'm a theoretical physicist fascinated by emergent phenomena—how complexity arises from simple rules. My work focuses on critical phenomena and complex systems, using statistical mechanics as my framework.

My interests span abstract mathematics, biological systems, and social sciences, always wandering across disciplinary boundaries. I support research through computational tools and AI, both as methods and as subjects of study.

As someone fascinated by collective behavior, I can’t resist the urge to join one myself: that’s why I do theatre. On stage, I explore the same questions that drive my research, just through complementary lens.

“Where the parts join, that’s where meaning arises.”
